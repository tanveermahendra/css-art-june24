Why was June made?—
Can you guess?

June was made for happiness!
Even the trees
Know this, and the breeze
That loves to play
In the sun all day,
And never is too bold or rough,
Like a child's laugh, a tiny bellow, a gentle puff;
And all the fields know
This is so—
June was not made for wind and stress,
June was made for happiness;
Little happy tulips dance,
And the meadow chants,
ACalling out when I pass,
"Read and rest here in the grass."
June was made for happy things,
Stories that rhyme to chirping birds whose wings,
Flap not in rain or stress,
June was made for happiness!

by Annette Wynne
